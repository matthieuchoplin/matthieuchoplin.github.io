Symbolic links

Matthieu Choplin

2014-06-04 22:38

Source

Find broken symlinks and delete them

$find -L /path/to/check -type l -delete

List them

$find -L /path -type l

If you want to delete them with confirmation first, do

find -L /path -type l -exec rm -i {} +
